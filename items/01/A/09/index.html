Vanz, Thomas. “Verse,” 2019

Immersive video exhibition.

This video installation takes a more fictional and emotive strategy in conveying the events of Big Bang, based in science but taking creative liberties on interpreting some of the more unknowable aspects of the universe. More on my thoughts on this work can be seen in The Archive.¹
