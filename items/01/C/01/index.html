A hundred flowers bloom

Handwriting practice books, 5 x 7 in.

This is one of my previous works where I reappropriate the Chinese handwriting practice book to reflect upon my own relationship with the country’s political and social history as well as the beauty that comes from learning. Some additional short thoughts about this project related to my current work can be found in The Archive.¹
