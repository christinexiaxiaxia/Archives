Paglen, Trevor. “Machine Visions”

Speaker event, 19 November, 2019.

After having attended Trevor Paglen’s speaker event, Machine Visions, I was reinvigorated with new inspirations in how to approach the idea of gatekeeping within documentations of people and archival practices. Paglen discusses the ways in which biases always linger within these global structures of technological development and scientific study, despite how infallibly accurate and objective they may seem. This topic of ways in which the study and categorization of people led me to the critical look at anthropology and ethnography within my Grave Eternity project. More about my thoughts on this project can be found in The Archive.¹
