Taxidermy business websites

Website links of various local business websites around the GTA, Ontario, and Canada.

I began my research into the possibilities of connecting the concept of an ‘archive of feelings’ with taxidermy by searching locally on local taxidermy business websites. I hoped to familiarize myself beyond my surface-level understanding of taxidermy and wanted to explore more of the current colloquial culture and terms used within the space. Beyond the insanely early-2000s web design of most of the sites I came across, I repeatedly encountered the term ‘trophy’ and the idea of bringing your well-earned prize back to life. These website were also so quiet and intimate, hidden away in the corners of the Web and some times with so many deeply personal stories attached to them. More details about my thoughts can be found in The Archive along with some subsequent (in progress) work.¹
