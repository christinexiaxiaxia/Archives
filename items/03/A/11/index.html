Photographers and documentation

Website links to the works of photographers.

After a fruitful discussion in class, I was provided with a list of photographers (Richard Avadon, Shelby Lee-Adams, Stacy Kranitz, Gordon Parks, and Lynne Cohen) whose works revolve around different approaches to documentary practices, all stepping away from the sterile ethnographic lens of the expedition portrait I collected. The works of these artists helped me open up to approaches outside of my comfort zone (with photography not being my outcome of choice for anything) to apply to my Essentially Anonymous project.
