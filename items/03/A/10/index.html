KesselsKramer. “Small Change”

Photobook.

Similar to their In Almost Every Picture book series, this book recreates the found images of how online auction website have found peculiar ways to censor the photographs in the items they sell. I find this observation and re-presentation of this necessary and vernacular design choice of censorship-through-currency to be a great (and more playful way) in which I can approach the topic of visibility and the control of documentation within my Essentially Anonymous project. More of my thoughts on the project can be found in The Archives.¹
