“Essentially Anonymous”, draft

Digital images.

Here is one of my first passes at trying to transform the expedition portrait I collected into a more humanized experience for the anonymous subjects of the photographs. While the current state of this draft is far from being realized, I think there is something interesting in the hidden/searching interaction that could be elevated.
