Colorized expedition portraits

Digital images.

I decided to try out an image colorization API as a possible strategy to try and re-humanize the expedition portrait I collected as well as some other images from the American Museum of Natural History’s displays and museum interiors. This attempt was quickly dispelled and I remain stuck on how to transform this collection of images. The full online collection of the expedition can be found here and more about this project and my thoughts can be found in The Archives.¹
