“Saving Grace”

Interactive website, sound.

This project was created using 6 selected audio tracks of Amazing Grace I collected from the Library of Congress’ digital archives. After having spent so much time sifting through the sterile databases of the Web, I decided to try and transform the valuable pieces of culture that some people had taken the time to document and catalog, and reinvigorate it with life and engagement. I ended up selecting Amazing Grace as the cultural artifact to bring back to life because of its expansive and tranformative history. The website is a cross-browser auditory experience where users are required to work together and maneuver the interface to mute the overlapping audio tracks to focus on a single one. In the same ways that Amazing Grace was able to shift and change its cultural value through the forces of a large population, the 6 audio tracks fade into one another with each user’ click to transform into a new melody altogether.
